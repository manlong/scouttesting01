About Us

99th Hong Kong Group Scout Troop -  Who we are


99th Hong Kong Group Scout Troop was established 1965, there were only 2 patrols at the begining. We adopte and operate Patrol System in the troop by providing an enjoyable, attractive and worth-while scheme of progressive training. Collectively, we have 7 leaders, 3 helpers and around 50 members in total. 


Our aim

Our aim is to encourage the spiritual, mental and physical development of the eleven to sixteen years old youths as an integral part of an overall plan of continuous training throughout the age ranges of the Movement.


Our training

The training of Scouts adopts a progressive training which are lead through a step-by-step learning process, in which their efforts and achievements are awarded and appreciated.


Our scarf

99's scarf is green and gray in colors to match with winter uniform of Raimondi College. 99's scarf adopted "rolled-up" folding method in earily days. As above folding method does not match with the top green and the bottom gray design, "folded and rolled" folding method has been adopted.
